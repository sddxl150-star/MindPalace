USDA and Palantir Launch Partnership to Deliver Faster, Modernized Support for Farmers

04 / 22 / 2026

Palantir enables the ‘One Farmer, One File’ initiative and is a key foundation of USDA’s IT Modernization

Palantir 推动“一位农民，一份档案”计划，是美国农业部 IT 现代化的关键基础

MIAMI--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) and Palantir Technologies Inc. (NASDAQ: PLTR) announced the signing of a $300 million Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) to support the National Farm Security Action Plan (NFSAP) and modernize how USDA delivers services to America’s farmers.

迈阿密——（BUSINESS WIRE）——美国农业部（USDA）与 Palantir 技术公司（纳斯达克：PLTR）宣布签署一份价值 3 亿美元的一揽子采购协议（BPA），以支持国家农场安全行动计划（NFSAP），并使美国农业部为美国农民提供服务的方式现代化。

The agreement advances two priorities: strengthening farm security as a national security imperative and accelerating producer-first program delivery across USDA’s Farm Production and Conservation (FPAC) mission areas.

该协议推进了两个优先事项：将农场安全作为国家安全必要事项予以加强，以及在 USDA 的农场生产与保护（FPAC）任务领域加快以生产者为优先的项目交付。

Building on capabilities already in use at USDA, Palantir will provide operational software to enable USDA to improve service delivery for American farmers and government field staff — and in doing so, secure the nation’s breadbasket.

基于 USDA 已在使用的能力，Palantir 将提供运营软件，使 USDA 能够改善为美国农民和政府外勤人员的服务交付——并在此过程中保障国家的粮仓安全。

Farm security is national security

农场安全就是国家安全

Through this partnership, Palantir is empowering USDA with core capabilities that will enable it to secure American farmland, enhance supply chain resilience, and shield agricultural programs from fraud, abuse, and foreign adversary influence.

通过此次合作，Palantir 为 USDA 提供核心能力，使其能够保护美国农田、增强供应链韧性，并保护农业项目免受欺诈、滥用和外国对手的影响。

In doing so, USDA will gain critical visibility into risks that can affect America’s agricultural production and food supply.

在此过程中，USDA 将获得对可能影响美国农业生产和食品供应的风险的关键可见性。

This agreement also supports USDA’s 'One Farmer, One File' initiative, which is cutting the red tape farmers face when accessing USDA services, delivering digital-first tools they can use from home, reducing time-to-payment, and accelerating post-disaster recovery.

该协议还支持 USDA 的 "一位农民，一份档案" 计划，该计划正在减少农民在获得 USDA 服务时面临的繁文缛节，提供他们可以在家使用的数字化优先工具，缩短付款时间，并加快灾后恢复。

This builds on existing work with USDA’s Landmark platform powered by Palantir, which backed the rollout of the $11 billion Farmer Bridge Assistance Program in February.

这建立在与 Palantir 提供支持的 USDA Landmark 平台的现有合作基础上，该平台在 2 月份支持了 110 亿美元的农民桥梁援助计划的推出。

Within 62 minutes of opening, the program broke all prior USDA records for online farmer sign-ups — allowing farmers to enroll without visiting a county office and delivering over $4.4 billion directly to farmers in the program’s first five days.

在开放后的 62 分钟内，该计划打破了 USDA 之前所有的农民在线注册记录——允许农民无需访问县级办公室即可注册，并在计划的前五天内直接向农民交付了超过 44 亿美元。

Landmark is also enabling USDA to transform how farmers report acreage through self-service digital tools.

Landmark 还使 USDA 能够通过自助数字化工具改变农民报告种植面积的方式。

For farmers, every hour of daylight matters — they shouldn't have to spend it sitting in field offices or driving far from their land.

对于农民来说，每一小时的日照都很重要——他们不应该把时间花在坐在田间办公室或开车远离他们的土地上。

Landmark gives farmers more options, enabling them to utilize county offices or self-service online tools according to their preference.

Landmark 为农民提供了更多选择，使他们能够根据自己的喜好利用县级办公室或自助在线工具。

Palantir is similarly empowering USDA's field staff with mobile digital tools that help them work efficiently, reduce administrative burdens, and accelerate services and payments to farmers.

Palantir 同样为 USDA 的外勤人员提供移动数字化工具，帮助他们高效工作，减少行政负担，并加快为农民提供服务和付款。

"America depends on its farmers, and USDA is moving fast to give them the technology they need," said Ali Monfre, Federal Engineering Lead at Palantir.

"美国依赖于它的农民，而 USDA 正在迅速为他们提供所需的技术，" Palantir 的联邦工程主管 Ali Monfre 说。

"They are raising the bar for what government can deliver for farmers, and we are honored to partner in that work."

"他们正在为政府能为农民提供什么树立更高的标准，我们很荣幸能参与这项工作。"

Underpinning these farmer-facing capabilities is a broader transformation of USDA's technology infrastructure.

支撑这些面向农民的能力的是 USDA 技术基础设施的更广泛变革。

The Landmark platform backed by Palantir is consolidating fragmented legacy systems into a secure, unified foundation — reducing maintenance burden, strengthening data security, and enabling USDA to move faster as new programs and priorities emerge.

Palantir 支持的 Landmark 平台正在将分散的遗留系统整合为一个安全、统一的基础——减少维护负担，加强数据安全，并使 USDA 能够在新计划和优先事项出现时更快地行动。

"Protecting America's farmland is protecting America itself, and this work gives USDA the visibility and speed needed to safeguard our food supply," said USDA Chief Information Officer Sam Berry.

"保护美国的农田就是保护美国本身，这项工作为 USDA 提供了保障我们食品供应所需的可见性和速度，" USDA 首席信息官 Sam Berry 说。

"Our farmers sustain this nation, and modern tools help us support them with greater precision.

"我们的农民维持着这个国家，现代工具帮助我们更精确地支持他们。

I look forward to working with Palantir as we continue serving the American farming community, which serves all of us every single day."

我期待与 Palantir 合作，继续为美国农业社区服务，而这个社区每天都在为我们所有人服务。"
